Energy Energy · Industry

While gas and power prices remain steady, mid-term supply imbalances loom due to potential disruptions and Saudi Arabia’s unusual oil import activities. Renewable energy investments have softened slightly, but ongoing Middle Eastern tensions raise the risk of acute supply constraints. Global energy sectors, including port logistics, refining, and construction, are exhibiting noticeable volatility.
