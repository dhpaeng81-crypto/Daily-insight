Finance Financial Markets

Following the announcement of the US re-entering the Iran nuclear deal and the reopening of the Strait of Hormuz, Dow Jones futures increased by approximately 0.7%, reflecting improved short-term investor sentiment in US equities. Technology and consumer discretionary sectors outperformed, while energy and defense stocks underperformed amid easing geopolitical tensions. The stock market experienced a turnaround buoyed by reduced geopolitical risks and stable interest rate expectations, increasing risk appetite focused on large-cap growth stocks. Meanwhile, bond markets remained neutral, signaling cautious optimism. These patterns suggest a repositioning of global capital flows back into risk assets.
