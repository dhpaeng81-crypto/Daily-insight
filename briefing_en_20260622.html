Hormuz Crisis Spurs Energy Rally; AI Growth Stocks Like INGREDION & Samsung Soar

Middle East energy risks fuel commodity surge with AI tech stocks leading innovation-driven gains

💹 Finance 🤖 AI · Tech ⚡ Energy 📈 Stocks
Finance Financial Markets

Global markets rallied today amid heightened geopolitical tensions around the Strait of Hormuz, boosting commodity and energy-related equities. The US S&P 500 rose 0.8%, propelled by a 2.3% jump in the energy sector which outperformed amid supply concerns. Defensive sectors such as consumer staples and leisure declined by around 0.4% due to increased uncertainty. South Korea's benchmark KOSPI index gained 0.6% to reclaim the 2760 level, supported by foreign buying and strength in refining and shipbuilding shares. Short-term US Treasury yields increased marginally by 3 basis points to close at 5.18%, reflecting cautious risk sentiment.
