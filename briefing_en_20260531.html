Energy Energy · Industry

Oil prices surged over 3%, reaching $144 per barrel, following ExxonMobil CEO warnings about looming crude shortages and the potential for prices hitting $160 per barrel. The ongoing Russia-Ukraine conflict continues to disrupt oil and gas infrastructure, heightening supply insecurity. This environment is driving increased investor interest in LNG and low-carbon energy sources. Renewable energy demand remains strong in the US and Europe, while Asia sees growing adoption of hydrogen and wind power projects. Across the energy supply chain, price volatility and the need to enhance LNG and power infrastructure persist as dominant themes.
