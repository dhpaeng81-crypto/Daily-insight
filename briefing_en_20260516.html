Sectors like automotive, retail, and gold-related stocks experienced weakness, while semiconductors and AI-related stocks showed modest gains. Tech AI · Tech Trends

The global AI market is rapidly expanding, projected to reach KRW 330 trillion in 2024, an 18% year-over-year increase. AI application trends span from personal finance and video generation to massive expansion of data center infrastructure. Power demand for AI data centers alone is expected to surge 23%, driving investments in AI semiconductors, power systems, and cloud infrastructure worldwide. OpenAI’s launch of a finance-focused ChatGPT marks AI’s deeper penetration into fintech. Major Korean players including Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix focus on high-performance memory and AI chips, integral to global AI infrastructure growth.

Energy Energy · Industry
