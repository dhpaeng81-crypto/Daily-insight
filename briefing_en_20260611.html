Energy Energy · Industry

European Union household energy consumption declined for the third consecutive year, with natural gas comprising 29.4%, electricity 26.9%, and renewables 22.8% of usage in 2023. This reflects synergistic effects of energy-saving policies, elevated interest rates, and the broader green energy transition affecting both residential and industrial demand. Meanwhile, Middle East geopolitical tensions, including threats from Houthi militants and increased oil shipments through the strategic Strait of Hormuz amid the Iran conflict, contributed to weekly crude oil price fluctuations of around 3%. Energy equities internationally have experienced amplified intraday volatility as a result.
