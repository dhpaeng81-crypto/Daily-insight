Energy Energy · Industry

In global energy markets, Abu Dhabi’s state-owned Masdar secured a record $5.1 billion contract to develop the world’s largest integrated solar-and-battery storage project. International crude oil prices retreated by 1.2% to around $82 per barrel, influenced by former President Trump’s reversal of shipping fee policies and easing Middle East tensions. The European Union formally announced plans to triple its renewable energy storage capacity by 2030, targeting to supply over 44% of its total energy needs from renewables. These developments are stimulating expansions in related supply chains and boosting equipment and component manufacturers both domestically and internationally.
